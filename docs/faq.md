# Farm Fresh Produce – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

**Q: What products do you sell?**  
A: We sell fresh farm produce including Coconut, Turmeric, Yucca (Cassava), Sugarcane, Banana, Tomato, and Ladies Finger (Okra). Product availability may vary based on season and harvest.

**Q: Are your products organically grown?**  
A: Our produce is grown using traditional farming practices. While not all items are certified organic, we focus on minimal chemical use and freshness.

---

## Pricing & Availability

**Q: How do I know today’s price?**  
A: Prices are updated daily based on availability and market conditions. Please ask the chatbot for the current price of a specific product.

**Q: Are prices shown per kilogram or per piece?**  
A: Pricing units vary by product. For example, tomatoes and turmeric are priced per kilogram, while coconuts and sugarcane are priced per piece.

**Q: What happens if a product is out of stock?**  
A: If a product is out of stock, the chatbot will let you know. You may ask about alternative products or check availability for another day.

---

## Orders

**Q: How do I place an order?**  
A: You can place an order by contacting us via phone or WhatsApp after checking availability and pricing through the chatbot.

**Q: Is there a minimum order quantity?**  
A: There is no minimum order quantity for pickup. For delivery orders, a minimum purchase amount may apply depending on location.

---

## Delivery & Pickup

**Q: Do you offer home delivery?**  
A: Yes, we offer local delivery within selected areas. Delivery charges may apply based on distance and order size.

**Q: Can I pick up my order from the farm?**  
A: Yes, farm pickup is available during our business hours. Please confirm availability before visiting.

**Q: What are your business hours?**  
A: Our standard business hours are Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Hours may change on public holidays.

---

## Quality & Substitutions

**Q: What if an item I ordered is not available at the time of packing?**  
A: If an item becomes unavailable, we will contact you to suggest a suitable substitute or adjust the order accordingly.

**Q: How fresh is the produce?**  
A: Most of our produce is harvested within 24–48 hours before sale to ensure freshness and quality.

---

## Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept cash, e-transfer, and other local digital payment methods at the time of delivery or pickup.

---

## Support

**Q: Can I speak to a human if I have questions?**  
A: Yes. If the chatbot cannot answer your question, it will guide you to contact our customer support team by phone or WhatsApp.

**Q: Does the chatbot take orders directly?**  
A: No. The chatbot provides information on pricing, availability, and policies. Orders are handled separately by our sales team.

---

## Seasonal Information

**Q: Are all products available year-round?**  
A: No. Some items such as ladies finger (okra) and turmeric are seasonal. Availability depends on harvest cycles.
