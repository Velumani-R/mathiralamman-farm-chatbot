# Farm Fresh Produce – Policies

## Order Policy

- Orders are confirmed only after product availability is verified.
- Prices are subject to change based on daily availability and market conditions.
- Orders are fulfilled on a first-come, first-served basis.

---

## Delivery Policy

- Local delivery is available within selected service areas.
- Delivery charges may apply depending on distance and order size.
- Same-day delivery is not guaranteed and depends on order time and availability.
- Customers will be informed if delivery is delayed due to weather or operational issues.

---

## Pickup Policy

- Farm pickup is available during business hours.
- Customers are advised to confirm product availability before visiting the farm.
- Orders not picked up on the scheduled date may be cancelled.

---

## Substitution Policy

- If a product becomes unavailable after order confirmation, customers will be contacted.
- Substitutions will be offered only with customer approval.
- If no suitable substitute is accepted, the item will be removed from the order.

---

## Refund Policy
Refunds are provided only if produce is found to be damaged or spoiled at the time of delivery or pickup.

Customers must report issues within 24 hours of receiving the items.
Proof (such as a photo) may be requested.

No refunds are offered for changes of mind or delays in pickup.

---

## Quality Policy

- We aim to provide fresh produce harvested within 24–48 hours.
- Natural variations in size, color, and shape are normal for farm produce.
- Customers are encouraged to report quality issues within 24 hours of delivery or pickup.

---

## Customer Support

- The chatbot provides informational support only.
- For order changes, complaints, or urgent issues, customers must contact the sales team directly.
- Contact details will be provided when escalation is required.
